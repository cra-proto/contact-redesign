Canada child benefit (CCB)

The Canada child benefit (CCB) is a tax-free monthly payment for eligible families to help with the cost of raising children under 18 years of age. The CCB may include the child disability benefit and any related provincial and territorial programs.

The CCB is administered by the Canada Revenue Agency (CRA).
